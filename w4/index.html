Fun facts about Illa Topuria


  • Multi-sport athlete: Before transitioning to MMA, Topuria was a talented wrestler and jiu-jitsu practitioner. He has a well-rounded background in grappling, which made him dangerous on the ground and set him up for success in mixed martial arts.
  • Born in Georgia, representing Spain: Ilya was born in Georgia (the country, not the state), but he represents Spain in international competitions. He moved to Spain at a young age and embraced the Spanish fight scene, becoming one of the top prospects in European MMA.
  • Undefeated streak: As of now, he has remained undefeated in his professional MMA career, which speaks to his exceptional skill, discipline, and versatility inside the octagon.
  • A deadly striker: Despite his grappling pedigree, Topuria is also a dangerous striker. He combines excellent footwork with knockout power, making him a well-rounded threat in any exchange.
  • A rapidly rising star: Ilya quickly climbed the ranks in the UFC's featherweight division. His rapid success and dominance have made him one of the most talked-about young fighters in the promotion.
  • Knockouts and submissions: Ilya is known for his ability to finish fights in multiple ways, whether by knockout or submission. His finishing ability has earned him a reputation as a dangerous and unpredictable opponent.
