    Like a Dragon: Pirate Yakuza in Hawaii

    Like a Dragon: Pirate Yakuza in Hawaii is an absolute masterpiece that blends the chaotic charm of the Yakuza series with a fresh, exhilarating twist. Set in the sun-soaked streets of Hawaii, it’s a perfect fusion of breathtaking landscapes, epic pirate battles, and the series' signature over-the-top action. The main character’s journey through both the criminal underworld and the high seas delivers emotional depth while never losing that signature Yakuza humor and heart. The new combat system, with its mix of swashbuckling sword fights and street brawls, is fluid and satisfying, while the side stories are as bizarre and hilarious as ever. It’s a game that not only exceeds expectations but redefines what we love about the series. Fans will be hooked from the first punch to the final treasure!
