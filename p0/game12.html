Scarlet Nexus

  • Overview: Scarlet Nexus is a Japanese action role-playing game developed by Bandai Namco Studios. The game follows the story of two protagonists, Yuito Sumeragi and Kasane Randall, as they fight against mysterious creatures known as Others. The game features psychic abilities, fast-paced combat, and a deep narrative intertwined with character relationships.
  • Developer: Bandai Namco Studios
  • Publisher: Bandai Namco Entertainment
  • Studio: Bandai Namco Studios
  • Platform: Available on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, and Microsoft Windows
  • Release Date: June 25, 2021
  • Average Price: Around $59.99 (Price may vary based on platform and region)
  • Ratings:
    • Metacritic: 81/100 (PlayStation 5 version)
    • Steam: 74% Positive (Based on user reviews)
  • Important Features:
    • Fast-paced, combo-based combat using psychic abilities.
    • Two protagonists with different perspectives and storylines.
    • A compelling world with deep lore and futuristic setting.
    • Detailed character progression system and customization.
    • Strategic use of psychic powers during battle.
    • Anime-inspired art style and visuals.
  • Criticisms:
    • Some players feel the story pacing is inconsistent.
    • Repetitive combat can become monotonous over time.
    • Character development for certain side characters was limited.
    • Several bugs and technical issues were reported at launch, but patches have addressed some of these.
  • User Reviews: Few user reviews on platforms like Steam and PlayStation, but overall positive feedback regarding combat and visuals. Some criticism was directed towards repetitive gameplay.
  • Sequel/Expansion: As of now, no direct sequel, but the game’s universe leaves room for potential future content.
  • Genre: Action RPG, Hack and Slash, Sci-Fi
  • Recommended for: Fans of anime-style action RPGs, players who enjoy fast-paced combat, and those looking for a unique story-driven experience in a futuristic world.
