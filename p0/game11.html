Assetto Corsa

  • Overview: Assetto Corsa is a realistic racing simulation game developed by Kunos Simulazioni. The game features highly detailed car models, realistic physics, and a wide range of tracks. It is known for its attention to detail and the level of customization it offers to players. The game focuses on providing a more immersive driving experience with support for various racing disciplines.
  • Developer: Kunos Simulazioni
  • Publisher: 505 Games
  • Studio: Kunos Simulazioni
  • Platform: Available on PC (Windows, Linux), PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and in VR on supported systems
  • Release Date: December 19, 2014 (PC), August 26, 2016 (Consoles)
  • Average Price: Around $39.99 (Price may vary based on platform and region, with sales offering discounts)
  • Ratings:
    • Metacritic: 86/100 (PC version)
    • Steam: 89% Very Positive (Based on user reviews)
  • Important Features:
    • Realistic physics engine for an immersive racing experience.
    • Wide selection of cars from various categories, including GT, Formula, and touring cars.
    • Extensive modding support, allowing players to create and download custom content.
    • Multiplayer racing with both competitive and casual modes.
    • Real-world racing circuits and custom-made tracks.
    • VR support for an even more immersive experience.
  • Criticisms:
    • Some players report occasional difficulty with AI behavior, particularly in single-player modes.
    • The user interface is considered by some to be clunky and less intuitive than other racing games.
    • Lack of certain features like a proper career mode compared to other racing simulators.
    • Requires a powerful PC setup for maximum performance, especially with mods and VR.
  • User Reviews: Very positive overall, with users praising the game’s realism, physics, and modding community. However, some criticize the lack of a structured career mode and the difficulty in handling AI races.
  • Sequel/Expansion: Assetto Corsa Competizione, a more focused and specialized sequel, mainly focusing on GT racing and esports. Assetto Corsa continues to receive updates and DLC packs.
  • Genre: Racing Simulation, Sports
  • Recommended for: Racing enthusiasts and simulation fans who appreciate realistic driving physics, car customization, and a wide variety of racing options.
